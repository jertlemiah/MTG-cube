B.R.E.A.D.S.

BREADS is an acronym that helps to prioritize what cards you should be selecting throughout the cube (try to pick cards that are higher on the list).

BREADS stands for:

  • Bombs - Big cards that will win you the game

  • Aggro - Creatures cards that represent an early threat

  • (Synergy) - Cards that are better than bombs with support

Bombs - Big cards that will win you the game
